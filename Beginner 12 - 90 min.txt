6 Dynamic Stretch Without Basketballs
Description: Dynamic stretching and warm-up
routine without using basketball
•Proper form •Technique
8 Form Shooting (Ch. 1 & 2)
Description: 1handed shooting then add the guide
hand
•Proper form •Eyes Up •Shooting foot
slightly in front •Start in athletic position
8 Forward/Reverse Pivots(Ch. 8 & 9)
Description: Forward and reverse pivots off
quickstops
•Staying Low •Staying on balance
5 1v1 Closeouts on Touch, 1v1 Closeouts
Description: Practicing closeout technique with an
offensive player trying to keep them out of the lane.
Start by going live on the touch, then go live on the
pass
•Weight back •Chop feet •Hands up
6 Change Of Directions Moves (Ch. 8,9,10,11)
Description: Crossover, behind the back, between
the legs, and inside-out moves on the go
•Eyes up •Going fast (mistakes are ok)
6 Passing: Chest, Bounce, Overhead, Baseball
Description: Spend 60-90 seconds each stationary
passing drills
•Passing on a line •Passing accurately
7 Layups: 1 Dribble (Ch. 2,3,&4)
Description: Lay up finishing techniques from both
right and left sides using 1 dribble -from 1st
hash,elbow, 3 point line (may need 2 dribbles
depending upon size/age)
•Ripping ball to the ear •Using
backboard •Proper footwork
6 Game Shots
Description: Shooting jump shots from 7’-10’ after
dribbling to perimeter and pivoting
•Quick stop •Shooting foot slightly in
front •Dribble out fast
