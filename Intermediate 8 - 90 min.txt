Pre-Practice Notes
Practice Session
Time(Min.) Drill Points of Emphasis
2 Character Message
6 Dynamic Stretching - Warm Up
Description: Dynamic stretching and warm-up routine
-progress through as many as possible
•Proper form •Proper technique
5 Form Shooting (Ch.1 & 2) and Form Shooting - 5 Lines
Description: Start 1 hand shooting and add guide hand,
working on shooting technique. For 5 Line, take ½ step
back after 2 makes
•Consistent technique •Proper
form •Elbow finishing above the
eye •Hold the follow through
5 Change Of Direction Moves -Crossover, Behind The Back,
Between The Legs, Inside Out (Ch. 8,9,10,11,12)
Description: On the move change of directions moves using
cones (can substitute items if cones unavailable) -
crossover, behind the back, between the legs, and inside
out, inside out w/ double move
•Change speeds •Eyes up •Keep
the ball low and crossover hard
•Go hard,mistakes are ok
5 Line Passing
Description: Practice different passing techniques: flick
pass, chest pass, bounce pass, overhead pass
•Proper technique
5 Finishing: Lay Ups (Ch 4) and ½ Court Lay Ups
Description: Lay up finishing techniques from both right
and left sides - 1 dribble from 3 point line and multiple
dribbles from ½ court
•Square to backboard •Keep the
ball high and away from defender
•Attacking the rim hard
5 1v1 Foster Drill
Description: Speed dribble around a cone and try to
beat defender to the basket
•Dribble fast •Attack the basket
•Read the defender
5 3v3 X Out Closeouts
Description: Make a long closeout into a 3v3 game -
limit dribbles to 0 or 1
•Sprint to closeout •Stay low •Stay
on balance
5 Monkey In The Middle Passing •Using pass fakes
Practice Plans: Intermediate #8 90 Minutes
Description: Live passing drill in groups of 3s with a
defense trying to tip the pass
•Sharp,crisp,direct passes
•Look opposite of the pass
8 4v4 Shell Defense
Description: Focus on defensive positioning and
defensive play -can be done 5v5 also
•Helpside defense •Moving while
ball is in the air •Proper alignment
defensively
4 Game Shots
Description: Dribble out to perimeter and use a quick
stop and pivot into an 8’-12’ jump shot
•Finishes misses •Dribble out
faster as you progress •Pivot right
and left into your shot
4 Bull in the Ring
Description: Box out and try to keep offense from
getting to the basketball
•Make first contact •Arms and
elbows out wide •Stay low
4 Dematha Finishing
Description: 1v1 finishing drill starting at the elbow -
finish off 1 or 2 feet
•Attacking the rim •Finishing off 1
or 2 feet •Finishing through
contact
5 3v3 No Dribble and 2 Dribbles
Description: Motion offense 3v3 starting with no
dribbles and progressing to 2 dribbles
•Cutting hard •Facing up to the
basket •Calling for the ball
10 5v5 5 Cut - Limit Dribble
Description: 5 out motion offense- pass and cut only -
no dribbles first 4 minutes and them unlimited dribbles
last 6 minutes
•Proper spacing •Read defense
and make correct cut •Squaring up
to the basket
5 2v1 Halfcourt Break
Description: Start at ½ court with 2 offense v 1 defense
•Reading the defense and making
the correct pass •Finishing at the
rim
6 3 Person/2 Ball Shooting
Description: Rebound your shot, pass out, and follow
your pass to perimeter for another shot
•Preparing early to shoot •Sprint
to your spot •Staying within your
shooting range
