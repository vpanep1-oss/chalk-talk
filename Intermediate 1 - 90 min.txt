6 Dynamic Stretching - Warm Up
Description: Dynamic stretching and warm-up routine
-progress through as many as possible
•Proper form •Proper technique

Description: Start 1 hand shooting,add guide hand, then
from shot pocket
•Proper form •Shooting foot

position • Hold follow through

Description: Layup finishing techniques from both right
and left sides - 1dribble from elbow and 1 dribble from 3

ball away from defender •Finish

Description: Closing out hard to the perimeter and not
giving up the paint on a drive
•Throw weight back •Hands up
•Stay on balance
5 Monkey In The Middle Passing
Description: Live passing drill in groups of 3s with a
defense trying to tip the pass
•Using pass fakes
•Sharp,crisp,direct passes
•Look opposite of the pass
10 4v4 Shell Defense
Description: Focus on defensive positioning and
defensive play -can be done 5v5 also
•Helpside defense •Moving while

defensively

Between The Legs, Inside Out (Ch. 8,9,10,11)
Description: On the move change of directions moves using
cones (can substitute items if cones unavailable) -
crossover, behind the back, between the legs, and inside
out
•Change speeds •Eyes up •Keep
the ball low and crossover hard
•Go hard,mistakes are ok
