6 Dynamic Stretch Without Basketballs
Description: Dynamic stretching and warm-up
routine without using basketball

8 Form Shooting (Ch.1, 2, 3)
Description: Start 1 hand and add guide hand as
well as starting from shot pocket
•Proper form •Going Fast •Shooting
foot slightly in front •Start in athletic
position • Hold follow through
8 Forward/Reverse Pivots (Ch. 8 & 9)
Description: Forward and reverse pivots off
quickstops
•Staying Low •Staying on balance
5 1v1 Closeouts
Description: Closing out to an offensive player and
playing live 1v1 (limit dribbles 2 or 3), defense is trying
to keep offense out of the paint
•Chop feet •Hand high and weight back
•NO LANE!
6 Change Of Directions Moves (Ch. 8,9,10,11)
Description: Crossover, behind the back, between
the legs, and inside-out moves on the go
•Eyes up •Going fast (mistakes are ok)
5 Speed Dribbling (Ch1,2,3)
Description: Begin working on speed dribbling
skills with a quick stop and switching hands each
dribble (Duke Speed Dribbling)
•Eyes up •Body in an athletic positions
•Push the ball out in front •Go FAST
6 Defense 4v4 Shell Cutters
Description: 4v4 Defensive shell drill working on
defensive positioning
•On ball defense •Gap defense •Help
side defense •Seeing ball at all times
7 Layups: 1 Dribble (Ch. 2 & 3) and Layups ½ Ct.
Description: Lay up finishing techniques from both
right and left sides using 1 dribble from first hash
and elbow and multiple dribbles from ½ co
